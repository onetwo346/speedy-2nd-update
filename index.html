Before You Pay — Refund Policy

Please read and accept before continuing to payment.

When refunds apply

Refunds are only issued if an item you ordered is unavailable at the store after our driver has already arrived to shop for you.

Refund amount

If you qualify for a refund, you will not be refunded 100% of that item's cost. Speedy Delivery retains 10% of the refunded amount to cover the driver's gas and time spent traveling to the store.

Delivery fee

The GH₵25 flat delivery fee is non-refundable once a driver has been dispatched.
